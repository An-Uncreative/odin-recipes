Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are a classic side dish that pairs well with a variety of main courses. They are creamy, buttery, and delicious, making them the perfect comfort food. Serve mashed potatoes with roast chicken, meatloaf, or grilled steak for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  4. Heat the milk and butter in a small saucepan until the butter is melted.
  5. Add the milk mixture, sour cream, salt, and pepper to the potatoes.
  6. Mash the potatoes with a potato masher until smooth and creamy.
  7. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
